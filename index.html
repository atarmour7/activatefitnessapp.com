The System

How It
Works

A simple loop that builds healthy habits without constant battles.

01
👤

Parent Sets Up

Create your account, add your child's profile, and set a daily step goal. Choose which apps to restrict when the balance hits zero — individual apps or whole categories like Games or Social Media.

02
📱

Pair the Device

Install Activate Fitness on your child's iPhone. Enter the 6-digit pairing code shown on your parent dashboard. Grant HealthKit and Screen Time access — and the system is live.

03
🏃

Kid Gets Moving

The child's iPhone automatically tracks steps via Apple HealthKit throughout the day. When they reach their step goal, screen time is instantly added to their balance. Walk more, earn more.

04
🎮

Unlock Screen Time

With minutes in their balance, the child holds the play button to unlock their apps. A live countdown shows exactly how much time remains. When it hits zero, apps are automatically blocked again.

Questions

Frequently
Asked

What devices does Activate Fitness work on?
Activate Fitness requires iPhone running iOS 17 or later. Both the parent's device and the child's device need the app installed. The Screen Time API and HealthKit features require iOS 17+.
How does step tracking work?
We use Apple HealthKit to read your child's daily step count directly from their iPhone's built-in motion sensor. No additional hardware or wearable is needed. The step count syncs throughout the day automatically.
Can my child bypass the app restrictions?
Activate Fitness uses Apple's official Screen Time API — the same technology Apple uses in built-in parental controls. Like any parental control system, a determined child with the device passcode could potentially bypass it, which is why we recommend keeping passcodes private and using iOS's own Screen Time passcode as a secondary layer.
What happens to screen time at midnight?
Screen time balances reset to zero at midnight each day. Any unused minutes do not carry over. Step counts also reset daily. This encourages consistent daily activity rather than banking time.
What if my child walks a lot but I want to cap their total screen time?
You can use the activity goal generator to set a "max daily screen time" — this is used to calculate how many minutes per goal your child earns, effectively setting an implicit cap. You can also use Scheduled Lockouts to enforce hard cutoffs at bedtime regardless of remaining balance.
Can I manage more than one child?
Yes. You can add multiple children to your parent account, each with their own step goals, app restrictions, schedules, and paired device. Each child's settings and balance are completely independent.
How do I delete my account or my child's data?
You can delete a child's profile directly from the app, and you can delete your entire parent account and all associated data in-app from your Account settings ("Delete Account") — removal from our servers completes within 30 days. Prefer email? Contact us at andrew.armour@activatefitnessapp.com and we'll process the request for you. We comply with COPPA and honor all deletion requests promptly.
Is Activate Fitness free?
Yes, Activate Fitness is currently completely free to download and use. If paid features are introduced in the future, you will receive at least 30 days advance notice and can choose whether to upgrade. No charges without your explicit consent.
What if the Screen Time permission gets revoked?
If your child (or anyone else) revokes the Screen Time permission from within iOS Settings, the app can no longer enforce restrictions. You'll receive a push notification alerting you, so you can restore the permission. We recommend keeping your iOS Screen Time passcode enabled as an additional safeguard.
Does the app work during bedtime or school hours?
During scheduled lockout windows (bedtime or school hours), apps are blocked regardless of your child's screen time balance. The lockout screen explains why apps are blocked and when they'll be accessible again. Lockouts are enforced by iOS at the system level.
How do I unpair a device?
You can unpair a device from the child's profile in your parent dashboard. After unpairing, the child's device returns to normal — all app restrictions are lifted and the app can be deleted. Parent verification (PIN) is required to unpair.
Does Activate Fitness sell data or show ads?
Absolutely not. We do not sell any data, run ads, or use behavioral tracking of any kind — especially for children's data. Activate Fitness is funded entirely by the App Store and optional future premium features. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.
How do I add the Home Screen widget?
Long press on your Home Screen until the apps start wiggling, tap the "+" button in the top left corner, search for "Activate Fitness", and choose the Medium size. This widget shows your Screen Time balance and step progress at a glance — no need to open the app! The widget tutorial also appears automatically after pairing setup.
How do I delete the app from my child's device?
When parental controls are active, iOS prevents the app from being deleted to protect your child's restrictions. To delete the app: open Activate Fitness on the child's device, tap the gear icon, select "Unpair This Device", and enter your parent PIN. Once unpaired, all restrictions are lifted and the app can be deleted normally from the Home Screen.
Can my child delete the app to bypass restrictions?
No. While parental controls are active, Activate Fitness uses Apple's denyAppRemoval feature to prevent the app from being deleted. The "Delete App" option is grayed out on the child's device. Only a parent with the PIN can unpair the device and allow deletion. This ensures your child's restrictions remain in place.
